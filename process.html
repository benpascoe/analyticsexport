Adding a Google Analytics User

How to enable api access

In order to export your Google Analytics data, we need viewer access to your Google Analytics profile.
Follow this short, step by step guide to provide access.

1: View Access Management

Go to the admin section of Google Analytics, select the view that you want to export data from and click on "View Access Management"

2: Click +

Click the plus button in the top right of the "View Access Management" window

3: Add Users

Click "Add users" in the pop up menu

4: Add Roles

Type the email address export@analyticsexport.com into the text box, it will recognise the account and show the Analytics Export logo.
Leave the "Notify new users by email" checkbox checked and the default "Viewer" role selected.
Click the Add button in the top right of the screen.

5: All done

Confirm that export@analyticsexport.com has been added as a viewer, and you're all done.
We will now confirm that we have access and start exporting your data, creating your report, and preparing your transfer.
